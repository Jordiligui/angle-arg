The Hounds of Tindalos

The angles are not safe.
They are the teeth of infinity,
biting at the boundaries of your perception.

They come from the past and the future,
from the corners that never were.
Time bends for them, and your seconds are their hunting ground.

Only the foolish linger in sharp angles.
The hounds wait there. Silent. Patient.
Every corner you avoid is a mercy.
