ANGLE 3 — THE FOLD

You’ve followed the angles this far.
But you’ve probably felt it by now:
The space isn’t flat anymore.

The echoes bend around you when you read these lines.
They always have — you just weren’t close enough to notice.

Every angle casts a shadow.
Some shadows overlap.
Some shadows hide things.

If you want to stay on the straight path,
then continue searching for the next angle.
It waits where it has always waited.
